About Me

My name is Laura, I was raised in Fort St. James, BC, growing up on the shores of Stuart Lake. I started volunteering at the Fort St. James National Historic Park when I was five years old, acting as a local native girl from the neighboring Nak'azdli Reserve. It was a fun experience, I was able to interact with tourists and fellow staff members, and I did this for two consecutive summers. This was the start of my varied work and life experience.
